School Psychological Assessment Referral Kit respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (Name, Email, Phone Number)
  • Student assessment details and referral information
  • Login credentials
  • Communication messages between counsellors and doctors

2. How We Use Information

  • To manage student assessments and referrals
  • To enable communication between counsellors and doctors
  • To provide parents access to recommendations
  • To improve system functionality and security

3. Data Security

We implement secure authentication and role-based access control to ensure that data is only accessible to authorized users.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Information is only shared between authorized school counsellors, doctors, and parents.

5. Data Retention

Student and user data is retained only as long as necessary for academic and assessment purposes.

6. User Rights

Users may request to update, modify, or delete their account information at any time.
